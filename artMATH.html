About the course

This course is first scheduled to be taught Fall ’15 at SHC. It will be cross listed ART 102 Drawing - MTH 183 Contemporary Math. Students will be able to select which course they get credit for.

This page is actively under development. Please email me if you have any thoughts!

Topics
  • lect1 :  Wanda - how to draw - measuring
  • technical drawing (enforces measuring and detail, drawings without perspective will make them want/appreciate  perspective when they get it the next week)
  • expanded gear works, screws, … characterized by measurements and scale (flat) drawings of the object from different profiles.
  • The next isn’t really a technical drawing but I’m a sucker for geometric object drawings.
  • Here are some from the 16th century:  
    • http://bibliodyssey.blogspot.com/2012/03/geometric-perspective.html
  • lect2:  Tony - (loosely leading up to perspective), infinity, briefly discuss the connection to religion…  
  • lect 3:  Wanda  - perspective checker patterns on floors
  • lect: Tony - mosaic tile patterns, tilings, symmetry
  • lect: Wanda - adv. drawing with perspective
  • lect: Tony -  Vi Hart - Fibonacci & pinecones
    • https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ahXIMUkSXX0&feature=kp
  • lect: Wanda - scientific drawing - natural high detail representations.  Sibley’s birds.  Butterflies. plants
    • http://edtech2.boisestate.edu/bretcrane/502/webquest/intro.html
  • follow how-to-draw guides: Flea, Mosquito, Salmon, Angel Fish, beetle, Crab
  • Send out into nature to do find own object.
  • lect: Tony - part II - hyperbolic space, escher disks, tessellations:
    • http://euler.slu.edu/escher/index.php/Hyperbolic_Geometry
  • lect: Wanda - drawing tessellations

    • misc other math+art topics:

  • origami  - http://erikdemaine.org/curved/

  • http://courses.csail.mit.edu/6.849/fall10/lectures/L01.html http://vimeo.com/47502276
  • Jordan curves  (short discussion on proofs and into to the theorem): perhaps the best Jordan curve could be done as an installation in salt:
  • http://www.motoi.biz/english/e_top/e_top.html http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Von_Koch_curve

  • other misc. topics:
  • Felice Varini projective geometry room   -- make room with tape on wall as a group.  draw results from different vantages in the room.   https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2_P-8rArDnE#t=47
  • shadow box --   https://anilaagha.see.me/yearinreview2013   make one and draw sketches of it
  • string shadows -- based on dexter - take two stands.  The top of one stand acts as a light source.  The second stand holds an object to be projected.  Run yarn from the ‘light source’ adjacent to the ‘object’ and tape to the wall.  The outline of the sting displays the boundary of the shadow.
