About the course
This course is first scheduled to be taught Fall ’15 at SHC. It will be cross listed ART 102 Drawing - MTH 183 Contemporary Math. Students will be able to select which course they get credit for.

This page is actively under development. Please email me if you have any thoughts!
Break down
Here is the basic percentage break down for the course. Below we have a tentative schedule. Each row represents a MWF lecture week. A typical semester at SHC consists of 14 weeks. However some weeks will be a day short. If a week is minus one day the show-and-tell/gallery day will typically be cut.

Course Tumblr :: 30%

Submit via Course Tumblr Submission Page.

Students are required to submit a weekly post. It should include some graphic/video with appropriate citation and a 200-400 word discussion about the work. Topics: (i) Piece of art or artist or installation, inspired by science/math, (ii) some math or mathematician which is visually appealing or artistic is some way, or (iii) some type of data visualization

Students are responsible for keeping a log of their posts, i.e. date, time and link to post.

Sketchbook :: 30%

Sketchbooks must be presented for review on TBA

The student is required to mantain a sketchbook with 10 or more entries a week related to the material of lecture that week.

Displayed works :: 30%

The art+math gallery is currently on display in TBA.

Students are required to submit a larger piece for public display (roughly once a week). Each Friday the class will view the gallery of our latest works. The last piece will be displayed during the final and will count double.

Participation :: 10%

Be on time. Stay the entire period. Be polite and respectful. Contribute.
