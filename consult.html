About
Occasionally I provide consulting, training, and freelance work. Contact me to set up an appointment. This first meeting is free. It is important that we meet long enough to define a problem, and decide if and how I can help you. Within 24 hours of this first meeting, I'll send you a written proposal outlining: (i) specific services provided, (ii) timeframe to completion, and (iii) fee structure.
