About the course
This is a series of lecture notes developing skills with interactive data visualization for the web. Our primary tool will be the d3.js library. As exemplary resources we will follow Mike Bostock bost.ocks.org and bl.ocks.org. Additionally, there is an abundance of online mbostock's list and print IDV4W sources.
