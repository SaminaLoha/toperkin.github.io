About
I am currently a software engineer with Redlion Controls. Previously a Professor and Department Chair of Mathematics at Spring Hill College. My current research interests are in potential theory, particularly its discrete versions with applications to sensor and resistor networks. I’ve also become increasingly involved in software development. Developmentally speaking my focus is on machine learning and artificial intelligence. In my free time I like to garden with my wife, Kathryn, and our dog, Hilda. I also volunteer with the Mobile Botanical Gardens where I work with the long-leaf pine forest restoration project. I am always looking for new projects to join. Please feel free to contact me with any ideas you may have.
